5 Interesting Facts About Einstein

No1.

He had difficulty learning to speak and did not fully learn to speak until age 4, and continued to have delayed speech for the rest of his life.

No2.

His second wife, Elsa, was also his first cousin.

No3.

He hated socks as an adult, and bragged in letters to his wife that he never wore them.

No4.

He had a passion for sailing.

No5.

He has five blood related great grandchildren who are alive today.
